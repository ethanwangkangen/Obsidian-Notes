- `auto` → "give me the value type, drop references." Safe default.
- `auto&` → "I want an lvalue reference, won't bind to rvalues."
- `auto&&` → forwarding reference. "Bind to anything, preserve category in the deduced type."
- `decltype(auto)` → "deduce exactly what the expression's type and category say, references and all." Used mostly for return types in generic wrappers.
